INDIA - GOLDEN TRIANGLE

Activities: Cultural tours, nightlife, jungle safari, visit to UNESCO heritage sites, typical Rajasthani dinner with cultural show.

Program

Day 1: Arrival in Delhi

Welcome to India - Welcome by our representative upon arrival at the Delhi Airport exit gate (gate # 5) and transfer to your pre-booked hotel, check in and check-in at the hotel. Delhi - capital of India and major gateway to the country.

Rest of the day free for leisure and overnight at the hotel in Delhi.

Day 2: Delhi

In the morning after breakfast, take a guided tour of Delhi and visit the tomb of Humayun, commissioned by Humayun's wife, Hamida Banu Begum, in 1562.Later, visit the highest Qutub Minar world brick minaret; it was commanded by Qutbuddin Aibak, the first Muslim leader in Delhi. Later, drive past India Gate, the neighborhood of President House and Lutyen before heading to Rajghat, the memorial dedicated to Mahatma Gandhi. After,

Evening enjoy the nightlife at the famous Pub & Bar (optional) Overnight at hotel in Delhi.

Day 3: Delhi - Jaipur by road (260 km / 5 hours)

In the morning, after breakfast, check out from the hotel, your driver will be available in the hotel lobby to get to Jaipur. Upon arrival in Jaipur, check in will be done at your hotel.
Jaipur popularly known as the pink city. It is famous for its colorful culture, fortresses, palaces and markets. The ancient city of Jaipur is painted pink, which gives it a magical glow. The rest of the day is free. Enjoy an ethnic dinner at Chokhi Dhani, an ethnic town complex with music and folk dances. Meals: Breakfast & dinner

Overnight at hotel in Jaipur.

Day 4: Guided tour of Jaipur

In the morning, after breakfast, we will continue the excursion to Amber Fort, we will take an elephant ride to reach the Fort located on the hill. Later, we will stop to photograph the beautiful Hawa Mahal, also known as the "Palace of the Winds". We will visit the City Palace, Jantar Mantar, etc. Next, we’ll walk through the city’s markets with Rickshaws and visit the carpet and jewelry factory.

Meals: Breakfast & Dinner
Overnight at hotel - Jaipur.

Day 5: Jaipur Day at Leisure

Free day, explore the local markets of Jaipur, here you can shop at the local market.

Day 6: Jaipur - Ranthambhore by road (180 km / 4 hours)

In the morning after breakfast, departure from the hotel and drive to Ranthambhore National Park. Ranthambhore, is not only a national park, it is an ideal home for tiger species and variants, a prime example of the tiger reserve in India. Upon arrival, check in.
Evening, enjoy the bonfire at your resort.

Night at the resort in Ranthambhore.

Meals: Dinner.
